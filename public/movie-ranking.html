Darren's Favourite Movies, Shows, and Anime

My Top 3-5 Picks as of 2024

Top Movie Picks:

Iron Man Series:

Iron Man is one of my all time favourite movies. The story and effects
of the first movie felt ahead of its time. My personal favourite suit
was the mark 6 with the new triangle arc reactor.

Pacific Rim:

Although the sequel to Pacific rim was a major letdown,
the first Pacific Rim was one of my all time favourites. It provided
many childhood memories and I continue to rewatch it every now and
then.

My Neighbour Totoro:

Totoro is another classic studio ghibli film that I enjoy. The art, story,
and classic Joe Hisaishi music has made this one of my all time favourites.
Not to mention the iconic Totoro character.

Top Show Picks:

Brooklyn 99:

Brooklyn 99 was one of the few shows aside from Kdramas that I
enjoyed a lot. Even with 8 seasons, I rewatched the entire show at
least 7 times.

How I Met Your Mother:

How I Met Your Mother was a show that I started during my trip in
Korea. Every night at the hotel I looked forward to relaxing while
watching a few episodes of this show before bed and it quickly became
a favourite of mine.

Top Anime Picks:

Initial D:

Initial D is the first anime that was introduced to me by my dad
and has been my favourite anime since. No other anime has taken
over the #1 spot for me. The introduction to eurobeat as well as
is something that I love about the anime.

Attack on Titan:

Attack on Titan or AOT for short is an anime I started back in High
but have just finished the last season recently. This anime had so
many hidden messages and foreshadowing that you can only notice once
you've rewatched it making it one of my favourites. The plot was amazing
and I enjoyed the sountracks.
